Accessing a list of forms

Note: Typically, CIG coordinators perform the actions described below.

To open the Forms tool, in the menubar, click Forms. This displays the Manage Forms page.

The Manage Forms page contains a list of forms that have been created locally for your site. The forms are ordered alphabetically. For each form, you can see the name of form owner, when it was last changed, and its site and global states (e.g., published, unpublished, or waiting for approval).

If the list of forms is longer than one page, use the buttons near the top of the page (First, Previous, Next, or Last) to display other pages in the list.
